Untitled

One hidden benefit of commuting through public transit and car sharing is time to read, write, and let your mind wonder. To people from fly-over states, this benefit is less felt and difficult to attain than thoses on the coasts. However, the impact is especially valuble to intellectuals and one that should be provided to everyone.

In 3.5 hours, I have read 50 percent of "No Vision, All Drive," wrote this blog post, and had processed thoughts I've been pondering on career and projects.
